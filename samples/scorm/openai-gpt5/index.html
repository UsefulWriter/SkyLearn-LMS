Open AI GPT-5 Fundamentals

A quick primer on GPT-5 capabilities, safety, and applications

Welcome

In this short course, you’ll explore GPT-5 at a high level: what it is, where it shines, and how to apply it responsibly.

What is GPT-5?

  • Large multimodal model designed for advanced reasoning and assistance.
  • Trained on diverse data to generalize across tasks.
  • Accessible via API for integration into products and workflows.

Key Capabilities

  • Natural language understanding and generation
  • Tool use and function calling for structured tasks
  • Longer-context reasoning and planning
  • Multimodal understanding (text + other modalities)

Safety and Alignment

  • Guardrails to reduce harmful or high-risk outputs
  • Content controls and policy enforcement
  • Continuous monitoring and red-teaming

Use Cases

  • Assistants and copilot experiences
  • Knowledge retrieval and summarization
  • Content drafting and editing
  • Automation of routine tasks

Limitations

  • May produce incorrect or outdated information
  • Needs careful prompt and system design for reliability
  • Policy and compliance considerations for sensitive domains
